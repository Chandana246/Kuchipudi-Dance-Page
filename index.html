Chandana Chittimalla

Kuchipudi Dancer

Chandana Chittimalla, a dynamic and multi-talented individual, hails from the captivating city of Warangal in Telangana. From a tender age of five, Chandana discovered her passion for Kuchipudi, a classical dance form, and began an extraordinary journey that would shape her into a professional dancer.

Having nurtured her love for dance for over 18 years, Chandana's dedication and perseverance have been unwavering. After completing her bachelor's degree in Computer Science from Vaagdevi College of Engineering, Warangal, she set her sights on expanding her horizons by pursuing a master's degree in Information Systems at Pace University, New York.

Throughout her journey, Chandana has had the privilege of being a disciple of several esteemed gurus. Under the tutelage of Smt. Vempati Nageshwari, she spent 12 formative years honing her skills in Kuchipudi. Additionally, Chandana learned from Smt. Vempati Sravani, daughter of Smt. Vempati Nageshwari, for a year, and later spent four years under the guidance of Smt. Kuppa Padmaja.

In 2020, Chandana embarked on an entrepreneurial journey by establishing her own dance school, "Laya Laasya Nruthya Kalakshethram," in Warangal. Despite challenges, Chandana remains resolute in her commitment to preserving and promoting Kuchipudi.

Chandana's journey as a performer began at the age of seven. Her performances in state-level competitions have consistently earned her distinctions and first prizes, highlighting her exceptional talent. Notably, Chandana had the privilege of performing in the esteemed "All India Music and Dance Competitions" at the age of eleven.

Her artistic endeavors extend beyond dance, including acting, dubbing, and painting. Chandana's versatility and passion for the arts are reflected in all aspects of her life, leaving an indelible mark on the world of Kuchipudi.
